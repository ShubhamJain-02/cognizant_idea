BlockInn

Hotel booking dApp

Book with confidence, earn with ease, BlockInn: where rewards meet transparency.

The Idea

The proposed dApp frontend enables users to search, view availability, and book hotel rooms. Bookings are conducted through smart contracts stored on the blockchain, ensuring transparent and secure transactions. Smart contracts define booking terms such as stay dates, room quantity, price, and payment details. Upon completion of the stay, the smart contract is terminated, and users receive cryptocurrency rewards based on factors like stay duration or room quantity. The dApp backend manages smart contracts, rewards issuance, and transaction handling, leveraging the efficient and scalable proof-of-stake concept and AI/ML algorithms bolster security by detecting phishing attacks and fake transactions. The user responses would be used to analyze user behavior and offer personalized loyalty offerings.
